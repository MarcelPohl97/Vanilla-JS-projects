Full functionality complete APP:
-> After starting you need to choose a name, you see a list of "rooms" you can join them they can be password protected or not
-> Once you are in you can see Kanban Boards which get updated in realtime when someone is working on them for example adding new tasks
-> they are draggable the boards itself and the task can be switched over to other boards and in the boards itself switched between the tasks 
-> you can switch the title of the board and when you add a task you can set a title and its possible to edit it and switch title or label color
